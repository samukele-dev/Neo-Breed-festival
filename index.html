WHAT IS THE NEO BREED EMPOWERMENT FESTIVAL?

A celebration of entrepreneurship, creativity, and community spirit featuring workshops, networking sessions, live performances, and the launch of the Neo Cash Cows.

Why partner with us?

Brand exposure, community engagement, and alignment with Neo Breed's mission. Customizable partnership options to meet specific needs and objectives.

Festival Highlights

Immerse yourself in the festivities with live music, workshops, exhibits, food stalls, networking, art displays, and family-friendly activities. We're expecting an attendance of over 10,000 entrepreneurs, creatives, and professionals.

The effect of the festival

Boosting local economy, fostering entrepreneurship, and empowering communities. Launching Neo Cash Cows, which are companies operating under the Neo brand aimed at serving entrepreneurs and neopreneurs.

Sponsorship opportunities

Brand visibility, logo placement, VIP access, hospitality, and workshop hosting. Tailored sponsorship packages to suit sponsor objectives.

How to get involved

Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities and benefits. Join us in making a meaningful impact on the community through entrepreneurship and creativity.
